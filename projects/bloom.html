TheBloomerie
Local Flower Shop

About

Bloomerie is a local flower shop in Vancouver. They hired the web-development agency I co-founded to create a business website for them. The main goal of the website was to showcase the products available. The client requirements were to create a responsible modern website with a lot of images. The project was completed in 1 month with only a few revisions at the end of the process. The team working on the website consisted of 2 people, my co-founding partner and myself.
